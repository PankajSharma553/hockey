Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our platform, Football Manager. By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. What data do we collect?

When using our platform, we may collect the following types of personal data:

A. Data you provide directly to us:

  • During registration: Name, email address, and username.
  • When contacting us: Messages you send to us via forms or email.

B. Data collected automatically:

  • Technical information: IP address, device type, operating system, and browser type.
  • Log information: Access dates and times, pages visited, and actions taken on the platform.
  • Cookies: Small files stored in your browser that help us improve the functionality of the platform.

C. Data from third parties:

If you register using third-party services (e.g., Google or Facebook), we may receive basic information from these accounts, in accordance with their privacy policies.

2. How do we use your data?

We process your data solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving our services.
  • Managing your account and personalizing the user experience.
  • Communicating with you (e.g., responding to inquiries, sending updates or news about the platform).
  • Analyzing and monitoring the use of the platform to optimize our services.
  • Complying with legal obligations and protecting our rights.

3. Sharing your data

We do not sell your data to third parties. We may share your data only in the following cases:

  • With service providers: Third parties that help us operate the platform (e.g., hosting, analytics).
  • For legal reasons: If required by the laws of the Czech Republic or if necessary to protect our rights.
  • In anonymized form: For statistical or research purposes.

4. How do we protect your data?

  • We use data encryption (e.g., SSL) to protect data transmission between your device and our servers.
  • We store data on secure servers with limited access.
  • We regularly update our systems and processes to comply with current data protection standards.

5. Your rights

As a user, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You have the right to request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: If your data is inaccurate or incomplete, you can request its correction.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your data if it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.
  • Restriction of processing: You have the right to restrict how your data is processed.
  • Data portability: Upon request, we will provide your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Withdrawal of consent: If you have consented to the processing of your data, you can withdraw your consent at any time.

To exercise your rights, please contact us at info@fotbalovymanazer.com.

6. Use of cookies

Our platform uses cookies for:

  • Remembering your preferences.
  • Improving the user experience.
  • Analyzing website traffic and performance.

You can adjust cookie settings directly in your browser. Some features of the site may be limited if you disable cookies.

7. Links to other websites

Our platform may contain links to external websites. We do not control the content or privacy policies of these websites and are not responsible for them.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If changes occur, we will notify you via a Avertissement on our platform or via email. The date of the last update can be found at the beginning of this page.
