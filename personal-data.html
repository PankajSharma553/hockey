Personal data

Your personal data is a priority for us. We are committed to protecting it and being transparent about how we handle it. This User Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data. We also strongly state that we never sell or rent your data to third parties.

1. Collection of Personal Data

We only collect data that is necessary to provide our services. This may include:

  • Data provided directly by the user: For example, during registration (name, email address, username).
  • Automatically collected data: Technical information, such as IP address, device type, and behavior on our platform (e.g., visited pages).
  • Data from external sources: If you register through third-party services (e.g., Google or Facebook), we may receive limited data in accordance with their privacy policies.

2. How do we use your data?

We process your data only for purposes that are clearly defined and legitimate:

  • Managing user accounts and providing our services.
  • Personalizing your experience on the platform.
  • Communicating with you, including responding to your inquiries.
  • Analyzing platform usage to optimize it.
  • Complying with legal obligations and protecting our rights.

3. Data Sharing Policy

We share your personal data only under the following circumstances:

  • With service providers: We collaborate with trusted partners who help us operate the platform (e.g., hosting providers, analytics tools). These partners may process data on our behalf but cannot use it for their own purposes.
  • For legal reasons: If required by law, we may share your data with regulatory or government authorities.
  • In anonymized form: Data that cannot identify you (e.g., aggregated statistics) may be used for analysis and research.

We never sell or rent your data to third parties.

4. Data Retention

We store your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes stated in this policy:

  • If you delete your account, we will remove your data, except for data we need to retain for legal reasons.
  • Automatically collected data (e.g., cookies) may be stored for a shorter period according to relevant settings.

5. How do we protect your data?

  • We use standard security measures, such as encryption (e.g., SSL), to protect your data during transmission.
  • We store data on secure servers with restricted access.
  • We regularly test our systems to ensure protection against unauthorized access or data breaches.

6. Your Rights

As a user, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a summary of personal data we process about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your data if it is no longer necessary.
  • Restriction of processing: Request a limitation on the processing of your data.
  • Data portability: Receive a copy of your data in a structured format.
  • Withdrawal of consent: Withdraw consent to data processing if consent was given.
